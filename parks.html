Walks in the Park

Ann Arbor's parks are a haven for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. Whether you're walking your dog or enjoying the scenery, these parks offer a tranquil escape from daily life.

Some favorite parks include:

  • Argo Park - Known for its river cascades and walking trails.
  • Gallup Park - A perfect spot for kayaking and birdwatching.
  • Bandemer Park - Ideal for picnics and peaceful walks along the Huron River.

Visit one of these parks and experience the beauty of Ann Arbor's natural landscapes!
